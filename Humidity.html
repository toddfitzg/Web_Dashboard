Summary - Latitude and Humidity


In this grapgh we are examining the effects latitude has on humidity

takend from over 2000 different city locations around the world.



Based on the table that is presented the humidity does not seem to be dependent upon the latitude


In order to understand how humidity is effected in different parts of the world you need to take into account many more observations than just the latitude.


Humidity is effected by tempurature and a number of other factors. An easy hypothisis is the closer to the equator you get the warmer the weather should be and thus more humidity.


Humidity is caused by how much moisture the air is able to hold in it. The movement of air from a high pressure system to a low pressure system.


In order to have humidity you need different pressure systems amongst many other things. It is amazing how all the different factors play together to help dictate the weather. Wind, Temperature, terrain, moisture availability all play into the creation of the weather patterns. Which goes well beyond just latitude.
